about

Last Gang Records is a music label founded in 2003, releasing the first Metric album ‘Old World Underground, Where Are You Now?’ in 2004. Primary operations are based in Toronto, Canada with offices in London, England and Los Angeles, California.

Last Gang’s core activities involve music production and distribution, international licensing, marketing and promotion, and overall project management.
